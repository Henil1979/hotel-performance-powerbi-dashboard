# Hotel Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyses hotel booking data to evaluate business performance, focusing on revenue trends, customer behaviour, and cancellation patterns.
The goal of the project is to simulate a real-world Business Intelligence scenario where data is transformed into actionable insights to support decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyse revenue performance over time  
- Identify key booking channels and market segments  
- Evaluate customer behaviour  
- Detect patterns in cancellations and revenue loss

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard Development)  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)  
- DAX (Measures & KPIs)  
- Excel / CSV Dataset

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong seasonal trends, peaking during mid-year periods  
- Online Travel Agents contribute the highest share of revenue  
- High cancellation rates in certain segments indicate potential revenue leakage  
- The business relies heavily on transient customers

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Reduce cancellations through stricter booking policies or incentives  
- Increase direct bookings to reduce reliance on third-party channels  
- Optimise pricing strategies during peak demand periods
